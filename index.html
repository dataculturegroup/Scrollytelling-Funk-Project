Pop Up:
A.a.Ron Myers and Ed Word Galan are the
dynamic duo behind A Trike Called Funk

A.a.Ron and Ed Word, along with collaborating artists, celebrate the diversity of pan-American dance styles throughout Boston communities, from Popping and Locking, to Salsa, Bachata, and Capoeira.

Our trike is a one-of-a-kind front-loading cargo tricycle converted into a mobile marketing vehicle and performance platform that's equipped with its own portable dance floor, power source, and DJ set-up.
